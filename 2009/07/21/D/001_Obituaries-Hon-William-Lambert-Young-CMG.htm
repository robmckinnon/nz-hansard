Obituaries — Hon William Lambert Young CMG

[Advance Copy - Subject to minor change before inclusion in Bound Volume.]

Tuesday, 21 July 2009

Mr Speaker took the Chair at 2 p.m.

Prayers.

Obituaries

Hon William Lambert Young CMG

Mr SPEAKER: I regret to inform the House of the death on 14 July 2009 of the Hon William (Bill) Lambert Young CMG, who represented the electorate of Miramar from 1966 to 1981. He was Minister of Works and Development from 1975 to 1981. I desire, on behalf of this House, to express our sense of the loss we have sustained and our sympathy with the relatives of the late former member.

Business of the House

Hon GERRY BROWNLEE (Leader of the House) : Following discussions among parties, I seek leave for there to be a question time at 2 p.m. on Wednesday and 2 p.m. on Thursday, despite urgency having been accorded to Government business.

Mr SPEAKER: Leave is sought for that course of action. Is there any objection? There is no objection.

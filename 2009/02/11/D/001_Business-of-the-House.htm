Wednesday, 11 February 2009

Mr Speaker took the Chair at 2 p.m.

Prayers.

Business of the House

Hon GERRY BROWNLEE (Leader of the House) : I seek leave for the debates on the question that the Government Superannuation Fund Amendment Bill be read a second and a third time to be taken together as one debate of 12 10-minute speeches.

Mr SPEAKER: Is there any objection? There is no objection.

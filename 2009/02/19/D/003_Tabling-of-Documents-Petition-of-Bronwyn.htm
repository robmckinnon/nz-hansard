Tabling of Documents

Petition of Bronwyn Holloway-Smith and 148 Others—Repeal of Section 92A of the Copyright Act

Hon PETER DUNNE (Leader—United Future) : As the sponsor of the petition just referred to, I seek leave to table a letter from Bronwyn Holloway-Smith advising that in addition to the 148 signatures on the petition, there were several thousand email signatures appended to it, which cannot be presented to the House.

Mr SPEAKER: Leave is sought to table that document. Is there any objection? There is no objection.

  • Document, by leave, laid on the Table of the House.

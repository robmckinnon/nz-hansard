Amended Answers to Oral Questions

Question No. 8 to Minister

Hon Dr WAYNE MAPP (Minister of Defence) : I seek leave of the House to correct a figure I gave in relation to question No. 8 today.

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: Leave is sought for that purpose. Is there any objection? There is no objection.

Hon Dr WAYNE MAPP: This afternoon I gave a figure of approximately $61,000. That was the result of adding two figures up in my head as I was standing. In fact, the correct figure, based on information supplied by the RNZAF, should have been $65,098. [Interruption]

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: There will be no comments.

Hon DAVID CUNLIFFE (Labour—New Lynn) : I raise a point of order, Mr Speaker. I appreciate the Minister’s comments. It is totally in line with the Standing Orders that he has come to the House to update it on that matter as soon as the information has come to him. I just wish the member to clarify. My recollection of question time is that he said that was the fuel—

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: No, no. That is not a point of order.

Hon DAVID CUNLIFFE: I just wish to clarify in respect of—

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: I am standing. The Minister sought leave to make a personal explanation. He has made the personal explanation. That is the end of the matter. We do not go into further debate on it.

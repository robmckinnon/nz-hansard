Business Statement

[Advance Copy - Subject to minor change before inclusion in Bound Volume.]

Thursday, 16 June 2011

Mr Speaker took the Chair at 2 p.m.

Prayers.

Business Statement

Hon SIMON POWER (Acting Leader of the House) : When the House resumes on Tuesday, 21 June the Government intends to hold the combined 3-hour debate on the second readings of the Appropriation (2010/11 Supplementary Estimates) Bill and the Imprest Supply (First for 2011/12) Bill. This debate will be followed by the consideration of two amendments to the supplementary estimates bill by the House in Committee. The Government also intends to make further progress on the Telecommunications (TSO, Broadband, and Other Matters) Amendment Bill.

Hon TREVOR MALLARD (Labour—Hutt South) : I will just check with the Minister: when he said “two amendments”, does that mean the two clauses that relate to Canterbury, or does it mean there is a further substantive amendment?

Hon SIMON POWER (Acting Leader of the House) : My memory is that they relate to Canterbury. If that is not the case, I will let the member know.

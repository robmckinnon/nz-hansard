Royal Society of New Zealand Amendment Bill

In Committee

  • Debate resumed from 3 August.

Clause 14 Elected Councillors (continued)

  • Clause 14 agreed to.

Clause 15 agreed to.

Clause 16 Co-opted Councillors

Hon HEATHER ROY (ACT) : I move, That the Committee report progress.

A party vote was called for on the question, That the Committee report progress.
Ayes 63 New Zealand National 57; ACT New Zealand 5; United Future 1.
Noes 52 New Zealand Labour 42; Green Party 8; Progressive 1; Independent: Carter C.
Motion agreed to.
  • House resumed.
  • Progress reported.

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: The question is that the report—

  • Report adopted.

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: This bill is set down—

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: The process here is that I have to put down first that the Royal Society of New Zealand Amendment Bill be set for consideration on the next sitting day. That is first process, then I can accept the seeking of leave. What we have done is adopt the report; that motion has been put. The next stage means that it cannot be debated today, so I am putting it down for further consideration on the next sitting day, as Standing Order 177(1) states. The member is seeking leave to depart from that. I am prepared to put that question to the House, but first of all I have to say that the bill is set down for further consideration on the next sitting day.

Hon TREVOR MALLARD (Labour—Hutt South) : I raise a point of order, Mr Speaker. I seek the leave of the House for the further Committee stage of the Royal Society of New Zealand Amendment Bill to be set down to be taken subsequent to the debate on the Education (Freedom of Association) Amendment Bill today, with no debate.

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: Are we all clear? Leave is sought for that purpose. Is there any objection? There is objection.

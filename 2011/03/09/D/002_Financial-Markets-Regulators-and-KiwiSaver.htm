Financial Markets (Regulators and KiwiSaver) Bill

Securities Trustees and Statutory Supervisors Bill

Procedure

Hon SIMON POWER (Acting Leader of the House) : Following discussion in the Business Committee yesterday, I seek leave for the Financial Markets (Regulators and KiwiSaver) Bill and the Securities Trustees and Statutory Supervisors Bill to be taken as cognate bills for their second and third readings, and that, for each reading, the debate comprise two 15-minute calls and 11 10-minute calls.

Mr SPEAKER: Is there any objection to that course of action being followed? There is none.

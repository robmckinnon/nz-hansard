Voting — Corrections

[Volume:666;Page:13339]

Tuesday, 24 August 2010

Mr Speaker took the Chair at 2 p.m.

Prayers.

Voting

Corrections

Mr SPEAKER: Honourable members, the result of the vote on the question for the first reading of the Employment Relations Amendment Bill (No 2) was incorrectly announced as Ayes 64, Noes 57. The correct result is Ayes 64 and Noes 56. Further, the result of the vote on the motion to refer the Taxation (GST and Remedial Matters) Bill to the Finance and Expenditure Committee was also incorrectly announced as Ayes 69 and Noes 52. The correct result is Ayes 68 and Noes 52. The record will be corrected in both cases.

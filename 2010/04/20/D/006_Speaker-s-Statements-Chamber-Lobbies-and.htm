Speaker’s Statements

Chamber, Lobbies, and Galleries—Audio System, Faulty Microphones

Mr SPEAKER: Before I go on to the next item of business—and I have received a couple of letters from members about this matter—I want to apologise again to members in the back quarter of the House on the Government side. The speakers were tested prior to the House sitting today, and they were operating properly prior to the House sitting. It seems that maybe something was disconnected when members came into the House. The technicians cannot come into the House at the moment to remedy that, so I apologise to members. We will just have to do the best we can. Members should feel free to move elsewhere if their own microphones are not working.

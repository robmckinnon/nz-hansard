Reports

Investigation into Securities Amendment Act 2002 Commencement Order 2009 (SR 2009/10)—Consideration of Report of Regulations Review Committee

John Boscawen: Mr Speaker—

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: I am sorry. The only person who can move a motion on this matter is the chair of the Regulations Review Committee, who is present in the House, or, in his absence, another member of the committee. I refer the member to Standing Order 247.

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 5 for consideration of the report of the Regulations Review Committee on the investigation into the Securities Amendment Act 2002 Commencement Order 2009 (SR 2009/10) was discharged.

Briefing on the Tamil People in Sri Lanka—Consideration of Report of Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 6 for consideration of the report of the Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee on the briefing on the Tamil people in Sri Lanka was discharged.

Investigation into the Copyright (New Technologies) Amendment Act 2008 Commencement Amendment Order (No 2) 2009 (SR 2009/51)—Consideration of Report of Regulations Review Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 7 for consideration of the report of the Regulations Review Committee on the investigation into the Copyright (New Technologies) Amendment Act 2008 Commencement Amendment Order (No 2) 2009 (SR 2009/51) was discharged.

Report by the New Zealand Delegation to the Australia - New Zealand Scrutiny of Legislation Conference: Scrutiny and Accountability in the 21st Century—Consideration of Report of Regulations Review Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 8 for consideration of the report of the Regulations Review Committee on the report by the New Zealand delegation to the Australia - New Zealand Scrutiny of Legislation Conference: Scrutiny and Accountability in the 21st Century was discharged.

Activities of the Regulations Review Committee in 2009—Consideration of Report of Regulations Review Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 9 for consideration of the report of the Regulations Review Committee on the activities of the Regulations Review Committee in 2009 was discharged.

Briefing from the Rt Hon Helen Clark, Administrator of the United Nations Development Fund—Consideration of Report of Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 10 for consideration of the report of the Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee on the briefing from the Rt Hon Helen Clark, Administrator of the United Nations Development Fund, was discharged.

Briefing on Bariatric Surgery and Its Benefits for Māori—Consideration of Report of Māori Affairs Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 11 for consideration of the report of the Māori Affairs Committee on the briefing on bariatric surgery and its benefits for Māori was discharged.

International Treaty Examinations — Agreement Between the Government of New Zealand and the Government of the United States of America on Science and Technology Cooperation Contributing to Domestic and External Security Capabilities—Consideration of Report of Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 12 for consideration of the report of the Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade Committee on the international treaty examination of the agreement between the Government of New Zealand and the Government of the United States of America on science and technology cooperation contributing to domestic and external security capabilities was discharged.

Report from the Controller and Auditor-General on Central Government: Results of the 2008/09 Audits—Consideration of Report of Finance and Expenditure Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 13 for consideration of the report of the Finance and Expenditure Committee on the report from the Controller and Auditor-General on Central government: Results of the 2008/09 Audits was discharged.

Reserve Bank of New Zealand’s Monetary Policy Statement, March 2010—Consideration of Report of Finance and Expenditure Committee

  • No member having risen, members’ order of the day No. 14 for consideration of the report of the Finance and Expenditure Committee on the Reserve Bank of New Zealand’s Monetary Policy Statement, March 2010 was discharged.

Sittings of the House—Leave to Rise Early

JO GOODHEW (Junior Whip—National) : I raise a point of order, Mr Speaker. I seek leave of the House, since we have made such fantastic progress this afternoon, for the House to rise early for the day.

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: Leave is sought for that purpose. Is there any objection? There is objection.

JOHN BOSCAWEN (ACT) : I raise a point of order, Mr Speaker. Can I speak to the report on the Reserve Bank’s Monetary Policy Statement?

Mr DEPUTY SPEAKER: No, I have already dealt with that. We are now on members’ order of the day No. 15.
